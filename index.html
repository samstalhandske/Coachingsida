Why get a coach?

Having a coach as a beginner lets you skip all the newbie form and programming issues. Everyone I began training with spent the first year or two getting lackluster results as we slowly learned.


Equipment quality questions? Ask coach. Pin pointing a weak point? Ask coach. Rehabbing an injury? Ask coach. Diet questions? Ask coach. Program questions? Coach. Water cut? Coach. Cardio? Coach. Need a spot on bench day? Coach.


Simply put; anyone who wants to maximize return on the time they invest into training as a beginner/intermediate should have a coach.
