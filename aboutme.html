Who am I?

  • 5 years of lifting experience, 3 years of coaching

  • IPF District junior champ in -105kg weight class

  • Coached >20 athletes taking them from beginner levels to advanced

  • Studied the musculoskeletal system, physiology, kinesiology and strength training at Karolinska Institutet

My name is Daniel Tomic Lindvall, I am a 22 year old IPF competitor and coach. My biggest ambition within the fitness world is lifting people up and helping to make them better.


I began lifting in high school, with my first two years being almost entirely wasted not having a clue about anything. After I hit my first 100kg bench I joined a powerlifting gym. Having elite athletes helping me out on a daily basis inspired me to start this coaching journey. I have first hand experience of most programming styles, I've coached elite level lifters at meets, gotten a gold medal in regionals and helped build up insane lifters over the years.


I began doing tons of research on everything from programming to kinesiology, and I started reaching out to beginners offering them free coaching. Over the years I developed my own style of coaching "in the shadows". I had a loyal base of clients that had gotten incredible results without really trying to sell my services in a public manner.


In 2020 I did a years worth of anatomy, physiology and training courses at Karolinska Institutet. I was planning to graduate with a degree in physiotherapy and medicine in order to boost my coaching. After a few practises at clinics, I realized physiotherapy was almost entirely about old people with arthritis, and not so much about ambitious athletes trying to become the best version of themselves.


I dropped out of Karolinska Institutet and decided to make an effort into publicizing my services. So here we are.
